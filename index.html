Who's the biggest boy group in the world?

Well, that would be Bangtan Sonyeondan aka BTS. The South Korean boy group consists of 7 members: Kim Nmajoon, Kim Seokjin, Min Yoongi, Jung Hoseok, Park Jimin, Kim Taehyung, and of course Jeon Jungkook. They debutted under Big Hit Entertainent on June 13, 2013 with their single 'No More Dream' on their album '2 Cool 4 Skool'and soon created numerous records since.
